Fish tartare

1. Dice the remaining tuna and place in a large bowl.
2. Add the plum, and soy sauce to the fish and mix well.
3. Add some topping (sesame, lime etc)
This is how you really do it

Steamed eggplant

1. Dice eggplants
2. Put eggplants in boiled water(0.5 cup).
3. Put salt and soy sauce in it.
4. Wait until water evaporates
5. Add the topping of grated raw dish.
